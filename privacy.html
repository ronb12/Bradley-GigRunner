Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 29, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using Bradley GigRunner, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the platform:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (hashed), phone number, and profile photo.
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location when you use the app as a driver or when you place an order as a customer (delivery address).
  • Payment Information: Payment method details for processing orders via Stripe. We do not store full card numbers — all payment data is handled by Stripe.
  • Order History: Items ordered, restaurants visited, delivery addresses, timestamps, and transaction amounts.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers for security and analytics.
  • Communications: Messages, reviews, ratings, and reports you submit through the platform.
  • Driver Information: Vehicle information, delivery activity, earnings, and payout details (Venmo, CashApp, Zelle, or PayPal handles for payout purposes only).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill food delivery orders.
  • To match customers with available drivers.
  • To process payments and driver payouts.
  • To provide real-time order tracking and delivery notifications.
  • To display your order history and account activity.
  • To calculate driver earnings, ratings, and performance metrics.
  • To send service-related communications (order confirmations, status updates).
  • To improve the platform, troubleshoot issues, and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as described below:

  • Between Users: Customer name and delivery address are shared with the assigned driver. Driver name, photo, and rating are shared with the customer during delivery.
  • Restaurants: Order details (items, special instructions, delivery address) are shared with the restaurant fulfilling your order.
  • Payment Processors: Payment data is shared with Stripe to process transactions securely.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for hosting, authentication, and database services. These providers process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data when required by law or to protect the safety of users and the public.

4. Location Data

Drivers' real-time location is shared with customers during active deliveries to enable order tracking. Location data is not stored permanently after the delivery is complete. Customers' delivery addresses are stored as part of their order history.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Order history is retained for accounting and dispute resolution purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), Firebase Authentication with secure token management, and Stripe's PCI-compliant payment infrastructure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Bradley GigRunner is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your account information and order history within the app at any time.
  • Correction: You can update your account information from your profile settings.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us.
  • Opt-out: You may opt out of promotional communications at any time.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use Firebase Authentication tokens stored in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell behavioral data to advertisers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, through in-app notifications. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
